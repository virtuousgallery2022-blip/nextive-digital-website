Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

At Nextive Digital, building trust through transparent data practices is a core value. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, use, safeguard, and potentially share the information you provide across our ecosystem consisting of Virtuous Gallery, Craftd.in, 22 Gen, and Bimaura.

2. Data Collection

We collect information directly from you when you submit a project inquiry, subscribe to our newsletter, or communicate with our teams. The data includes names, email addresses, phone numbers, and any company details provided in correspondence.

3. Use of Information

Your data is utilized strictly to provide our services, improve user experience, communicate project deliverables, process transactions, and selectively offer updates on Nextive Digital services that align with your business goals.

4. Data Security

We employ industry-standard encryption, firewalls, and secure socket layer (SSL) technology to protect sensitive data transmitted through our website. Our development standards via Virtuous Gallery and 22 Gen ensure strict adherence to international security compliances.

5. Third-Party Sharing

Nextive Digital does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to outside parties. Data may be securely shared with trusted partners essential to providing requested architectural models (Bimaura) or deploying marketing networks (Craftd.in) under strict NDAs.
